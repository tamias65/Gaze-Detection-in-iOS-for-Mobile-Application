sessionA:

10 videos of Head Pose and accompanying ground truth for Yaw and Pitch angles (in degrees)

sessionB:

10 videos of Head Pose and Eye Gaze with accompanying ground truth for Yaw, Pitch (in degrees) and eye gaze directionality (in classes), as well as timestamps (in frames) regarding each combination of Head Pose and Eye Gaze

for more details, pls read:

S. Asteriadis, D. Soufleros, K. Karpouzis, S. Kollias, "A Natural Head Pose and Eye Gaze Dataset", International Conference on Multimodal Interfaces (ICMI 2009), November 2-6, Boston, MA.
